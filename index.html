What is Fix!t

Fix!t is an annual debugging contest held by CSE , in which teams of 4 are represented with a series of errored codes with deficulties ranging from easy to hard . the errors could be lexical,syntaxic,semantic and vary in the domain , there could be math errors,sql requests errors binary manipulation errors....
